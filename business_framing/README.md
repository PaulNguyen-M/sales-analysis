# Module 1: Business Framing

## Overview

This module defines the business context, problems, and analytical direction for the sales data analysis project.

## Business Problems

* Lack of visibility into product performance
* Ineffective promotion usage
* No clear customer segmentation
* Data inconsistencies

## Objectives

* Identify key revenue drivers
* Evaluate promotion effectiveness
* Analyze customer value
* Detect anomalies

## KPI

* Revenue
* Profit
* Sales volume
* Promotion ratio

## Analytical Plan

* Product analysis
* Customer analysis
* Promotion analysis
* Time-based analysis
