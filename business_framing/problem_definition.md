# Problem Definition

The business currently faces several challenges:
1. Lack of visibility into top-performing products
    - It is unclear which products contribute the most to revenue and profit.
2. Ineffective promotion strategy
    - Promotion quantity (SL Xuat KM) is recorded, but its impact on revenue is not evaluated
3. No customer segmentation
    - The business does not identify high-value customers.
4. Data inconsistencies
    - Some transactions show nagative revenue or profit, indicating possible returns or data issues.

There problems limit the company's ability to make data-driven decisions.
