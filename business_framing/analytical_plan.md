# Analytical Plan
To address the business problems, the analysis will focus on:
1. Product Analysis
    - Identify top-performing products by revenue
2. Customer Analysis
    - Determine high-value customer and their contribution
3. Promotion Analysis
    - Compare transactions with and without promotions
4. Time Analysis
    - Analyze revenue trends by date and month
5. Data Quality Check
    - Detect anomalies such as negative revenue or zero sales

The findings will guide business improvements in sales strategy and customer management.
